About Us

Welcome to the University of Wisconsin Mad Makers!

We are a group of students at UW that love to create, design, and solve problems in our daily lives. We mainly operate out of the Makerspace located at UW. This space contains 3D printers, laser cutters, a workshop, and more. More info about the space can be found here! The main focus of the club is themed design competitions, but we also assist in funding personal projects being created in the Makerspace
